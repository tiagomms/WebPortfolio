Résumé

My name is Tiago Silva and I’m {{meYears}} years old.

Over the past years, I have studied Biomedical Engineering and Computational Neuroscience in IST Lisbon, Education Technology for Maths under Sugata Mitra in Newcastle University and a year in Software Programming at École 42 in Paris. I have also worked as an intern for 9 months at INOVAsis in Aveiro, Portugal, designing infographics and developing the frontend of a responsive WebApp for the Portuguese Group of Retina Studies.

The goal of this webpage is to display all the learning experiences I have had in an objective manner, since I entered university in September 2009 until August 2017. I used charts to display when and what project I was doing, how complex they were and how motivated I was while developing them, and what projects, learning/work places and skillsets they were related to. In addition, I included an ‘Inner Consciousness’ page to complement the descriptions of what I have done with how I felt at the time.

From this moment onwards, I look forward to getting more experience in frontend development in a place with a great collaborative work environment. My long term goal is to develop new ways of showing information, mathematical symbolisms and data to children, while getting them involved in the creative process.
