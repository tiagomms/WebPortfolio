The goal of this portfolio was to display in an objective manner all the learning experiences I have had since I entered university in September 2009 until August 2017. *This page will not be displayed correctly in IE10, 11 or current Edge. Please make you have the latest version of your browser installed. I used charts to display when and what project I was doing, how complex they were and how motivated I was while developing them, and what projects, learning/work places and skillsets they were related to. In addition, I included an ‘Inner Consciousness’ page to complement the descriptions of what I have done with how I felt at the time.

Portfolio Main Page

A timeline is displayed on top, with the projects and education/work locations’ description on the right side. Each line corresponds to a project/education/working/volunteering experience and it is represented by the color and character displayed on the links menu. Each coloured line and corresponding description are links for the project/education/working/volunteering experience’s page.

Descriptions of major events are displayed throughout the timeline in chronological order, with a vertical line crossing them. These lines are present in all charts, marking each event’s time period.

Skill charts display how complex tasks were (in red) and how motivated I felt (in green) during a given time period. Although it is a very subjective scale, it’s goal was to allow a more or less objective comparison of the motivation and complexity perceived for each task/event.
Additionally, I included an ‘Inner Consciousness’ chart to complement the descriptions of what I have done with how I felt at the time, using 'clarity' (in purple) and happiness (in light green) as indicators.

When clicked, each of these charts are zoomed in through a modal window. On top of the modal window, there is a link for a page describing the sum of the corresponding experience.

All Other Portfolio Pages

I divided these pages in 4 sections: timeline, description, notes and related skills. On the timeline section, the timeline chart is displayed. This chart is similar to the one on the main page, but contains only projects/education/working/volunteering experiences related to the page’s subject. The description and notes hold the information about the page’s theme. The related skills section displays all related skills and their charts.

Across each chart, there are 2 thicker vertical lines to depict the start and end dates of each experience. *In these pages' modal windows, a link is displayed only for the main skills (Web Programming, Design, Education, Mathematics, Other Programming) and Inner Consciousness.
When clicked, all charts are zoomed in through a modal window.

Additional Functionalities

In every page (except this one), when hovering over a timeline coloured line or its corresponding link in the list, they are both highlighted.
When clicking over an image, it is zoomed in on a modal window.
