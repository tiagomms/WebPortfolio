Developed at IST Lisbon (October 2012 - February 2013) for the module Biomechanics of Movement, a part of MSc’s in Biomedical Engineering [A]. This project’s goal was to develop a 2-Dimensional kinematic and dynamic representation of a real person’s gait cycle. A Graphical User Interface was developed to simplify the study of kinematic and dynamic movement of specific pre-determined body sections.

The workgroup was comprised of 4 students. I worked as group leader and MatLab lead developer, responsible for the Graphical User Interface and data plotting.
